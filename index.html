'Woori' means 'We' in Korean.
All programs at Woori Community Center are designed to promote community mental health by engaging the community.

Why We started

Mental health is a problem we need to address together

  • Mental health problems are common.
  • Stigma is associated with mental health problems.
  • Many people are not well informed and people often do not know how to respond to those in-need.
  • Professional help is not always on-hand.
  • People with mental health problems often cannot seek help on their own.
