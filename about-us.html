About Woori Community Center

"Let's care for our mental health together,"
We are the group of Korean-American Mental Health First-Aider (MHFA) advocates for promoting mental health awareness and providing resources to those in need

We provide and support culturally appropriate mental health counseling services and various MHFA programs for the underprivileged and isolated people in the community.

We plan and provide programs to enhance understanding of mental health care and improve preconceived notions about mental illness and mental health.

Training & Campaign
Learn the value of healthy mental health through regular Mental Health First Aid workshops.
Mental Wellness Programs
Provide educational and cultural programs for Mental Health First Aiders and bridge the opportunities to the community.
Youth Programs
Educate the next generation's leaders on maintaining their healthy mindset and enjoying life with a stable and positive attitude.
